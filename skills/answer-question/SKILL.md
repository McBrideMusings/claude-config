# Answer Question

The user asked a question. Answer it.

Rules:
- Answer in chat only.
- Do not edit files.
- Do not propose or suggest fixes.
- Do not list next steps or offer to implement anything.
- Do not re-propose a plan or summarize what you were doing.
- One question = one answer. Stop there.
